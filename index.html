About the Platform

Built out of Clinical Insight & Public Care Dedication

DentalLink was designed and developed to break down structural friction points in public oral healthcare. By combining direct operational insights gained working hands-on within high-volume surgical, endodontic, and foundational clinics in New York City with software development, this architecture provides direct patient tools to confidently navigate complex codes, minimize systemic language gaps, and discover accessible treatment choices.
